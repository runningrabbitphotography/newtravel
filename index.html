Rick Steves Tour Alps 2025

Janet and I took a My Way Rick Steves tour through the Alps over 12 days.

We started our vacation by flying into Munich, Germany. We stayed a few days and then took a train to Salzburg, Austria to begin the Rick Steves tour. It started in Salzburg, continued on to Castelrotto (Italy), Fussen (Germany), Lauterbrunnen (Switzerland) and ended in Chamonix (France). There, we continued our vacation by going to Varenna in Lake Como, Italy, ending in Milan, Italy.

Cruise Mediterranean 2024

Janet and I took a cruise through the Mediterranean on Oceania Cruise Lines. Specifically, on Marina.

The cruise stopped in France (Saint Tropez), Spain (Barcelona, Seville and Cadiz), Morocco (Casablanca, Rabat and Agadir), back to Spain for the Canary Islands of Arracife, Tenerife and La Palma, to Portugal (Funchal aka Madeira) and finally ended in Lisbon, Portugal. We stayed another week in Portugal, visiting Pinhao, Porto and Lisbon where we flew back to San Francisco.
